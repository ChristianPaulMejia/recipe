Recipe Title

December 1, 2017

Ingredients

  • 1-500g pack Sarangani Bay Bangus Back Fillet (cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 bayleaf
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 green long chilies

Other-info

  • Cooking Time: 30 mins
  • Serving Size: Make 3 servings.

Directions

  • 1In a clean bowl marinate bangus in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bayleaf and pepper for 30 mins.
  • 2Drain fish from marinade. Set aside marinade.
  • 3Heat cooking oil in a frying pan. Fry bangus till light brown..
  • 4Remove excess oil from the frying pan using a spoon. Slowly add the marinade and allow to cook for 5 minutes.
  • 5Stir in coconut milk. Add chilies. Allow to cookfor 2 minutes.
  • 6Remove from heat. Serve Hot
